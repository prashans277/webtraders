Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy applies to everyone using TradingGuru and covers how we handle payments, subscriptions, cancellations, and platform access. It should be read alongside our Terms and Conditions.

1. Refunds

Payments made for courses, mentorship plans, the Premium Community, or any other paid service on TradingGuru are final. Once a course or subscription has been activated and access granted, we don't offer refunds — including for partial use, change of mind, or simply not finding the time to complete a course. The one exception is where a Service is proven to be materially defective, wasn't delivered at all, or was significantly misrepresented at the time of purchase — in that specific case, contact us at support@tradingguru.com and we'll review it in good faith.

2. Cancellations

Once a subscription or course has been activated, it can't be cancelled mid-term for a partial refund. This keeps things fair and consistent across all our users — everyone is held to the same rule, regardless of how much of the content or subscription period they've actually used.

3. If TradingGuru Ever Shuts Down

In the unlikely event that we have to shut the Platform down abruptly, we won't be obligated to refund fees already paid for courses, mentorship, Premium Community access, or any other paid service. We'd aim to give as much advance notice as reasonably possible if this were ever to happen.

4. Limitation of Liability

TradingGuru isn't liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential losses arising from your use — or inability to use — the Platform, including service interruptions or a paid feature being temporarily unavailable. This applies to all users, including those with active paid plans.

5. If You Simply Stop Using the Platform

If you voluntarily stop attending sessions or logging in after purchasing a course or plan, no refund is issued for the unused portion. You're responsible for managing your own subscription and making use of the access period you've paid for.

6. Account Misuse & Shared Access

We monitor for signs of account misuse — for example, logins from an unusually high number of devices, or access patterns that look like a single paid account is being shared across multiple people. Accounts showing this kind of activity may be flagged, restricted, or suspended without a refund. This exists to prevent unauthorized sharing and to keep the Platform fair for everyone paying for it.

7. Blacklisted Accounts

Users who violate our Terms and Conditions — including through account sharing, payment fraud, or abuse of the refund process — may be blacklisted. This can mean permanent suspension of the account, loss of access to all purchased content, and being barred from creating new accounts on the Platform in the future.

8. Legal Action

We reserve the right to pursue legal action against users involved in fraudulent payments, misuse or abuse of the Platform, unauthorized redistribution of course content, or any conduct that causes harm to TradingGuru or its users — including reporting such conduct to the relevant authorities where applicable law requires or permits it.

9. Questions About a Payment

If something looks wrong with a charge, please contact us first at support@tradingguru.com with your order details before raising a dispute with your bank or payment provider — most billing questions can be sorted out directly and faster this way.

This page is a general-purpose starting template and is not legal advice. A strict no-refund, no-cancellation policy carries real legal risk in some jurisdictions and under certain consumer-protection laws — please have this policy reviewed by a qualified lawyer before your Platform accepts live payments.
