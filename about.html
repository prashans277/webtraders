About Us

Welcome to TradingGuru

We started in 2021 as a small Telegram channel where a handful of us posted our own trade journals and weekly watchlists for friends. Once the channel crossed a few thousand members, our pinned watchlist PDF was being screenshotted and re-shared so many times a day that half the group was trading off numbers that were already hours old. That's when we realized a group chat wasn't going to cut it anymore.

We built our first proper course site in 2022 on a simple template, rebuilt it from the ground up in 2024, and have been refining it steadily ever since.

Our goal is to make trading education feel structured and honest, instead of a pile of disconnected YouTube clips or a paid signals group with no real accountability. Today we cover stock market fundamentals, technical analysis, price action, risk management, and trading psychology, with new formats planned as we grow.
